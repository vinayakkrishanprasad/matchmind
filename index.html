Methodology

Open source · GitHub
01
Poisson Goal Model
Each team's expected goals are estimated using a Dixon-Coles Poisson regression on 4 years of international data. Attack and defense parameters update after each finished match. Scoreline distributions are simulated to produce win/draw/loss probabilities.
02
Named Entity Sentiment
Text from r/soccer, r/worldcup, and NewsAPI is processed with spaCy for named entity resolution — attributing sentiment to specific teams and players, not whole documents. Post karma and source credibility are used as weighting signals before aggregation.
03
Temporal Decay
Sentiment collected more than 72 hours before kickoff is discounted exponentially. Spikes within 6 hours — typically after team news or press conferences — are treated as a higher-weight signal class and tracked separately from ambient fan discourse.
04
Market Calibration
After each match, probabilities are compared against closing odds from the Odds API. Brier scores are computed for both the sentiment-adjusted model and the ELO baseline, giving a running measure of whether sentiment adds real predictive value across 104 matches.
